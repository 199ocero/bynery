### 2. Trail Wallet — Best for Daily Budget Limits

Trail Wallet is a simple, beautifully designed app for travelers who want to set a daily spending limit and track whether they are over or under. No receipt scanning — you enter amounts manually — but the visual feedback is excellent.

**Key features:**

### 3. TravelSpend — Best for Trip-by-Trip Budgets

TravelSpend lets you create separate expense logs per trip, track daily spending in local currency, and see a running total converted to your home currency. Popular with backpackers and leisure travelers.

### 4. Expensify — Best for Business Travel Reimbursements

If your company reimburses travel expenses, Expensify has the most polished submission workflow. Scan a receipt, attach it to an expense report, submit to your manager — all within the app.

### 5. Splitwise — Best for Group Travel

Splitwise does not track your personal budget — it tracks who owes whom in a group. Essential for shared accommodation, group dinners, and any trip where multiple people are splitting costs.

**Key features:**

- Multi-currency with automatic conversion
- IOU tracking and settlement suggestions
- Free tier covers most group travel needs

**Limitation:** Not an expense tracker — purely for splitting and settling group costs.

2. **Let the app handle currency conversion** — no mental math, no looking up exchange rates manually
3. **Review your expense summary weekly** — EasyReceipt's summary view shows where your money is going across the trip
4. **Export at the end of the trip** — one CSV or Google Sheet with every expense, converted to your home currency, ready for reimbursement or tax records

## Cash vs. Card Abroad: Why Receipt Scanning Matters More for Cash

When you pay by card internationally, your bank statement records the transaction — messy exchange rate and all. When you pay cash, there is no record at all unless you keep the receipt.

In many countries — Japan, parts of Southeast Asia, local markets across Europe — cash is still the dominant payment method. AI receipt scanning is the only reliable way to capture those expenses digitally before the receipt gets lost in your bag.
