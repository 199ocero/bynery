## What Makes a Great Receipt App?

In 2026, the best receipt scanner apps share these qualities:
- **Fast, accurate OCR** — scans take seconds, not minutes
- **AI-powered extraction** — recognizes items, not just total amounts
- **Cloud sync** — access receipts from any device
- **Smart categorization** — automatic organization by merchant type
- **Export options** — CSV, PDF, and integrations with accounting software
