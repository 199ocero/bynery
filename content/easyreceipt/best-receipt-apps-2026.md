## Why AI-Powered Scanning Beats Basic OCR

Most older apps use basic OCR — they photograph a receipt and pull out the grand total. That still leaves you manually entering merchant names, categories, and individual items.

AI-powered scanning reads the entire receipt structurally. It understands context — recognizing merchant names, currencies, and itemized purchases without human correction. The result:

- **Faster processing** — scans complete in seconds, not minutes
- **Higher accuracy** — less manual correction after the fact
- **Currency awareness** — automatically detects and converts foreign currency amounts
- **Cleaner exports** — structured data ready for spreadsheets or accounting tools

This matters most for freelancers, small business owners, and frequent travelers who deal with receipts across multiple currencies and need clean records at tax time.
