## What EasyReceipt Does Well

### AI Smart Scan accuracy

The standout feature is the AI extraction quality. Most basic OCR apps capture the grand total and maybe the merchant name — EasyReceipt pulls line items, quantities, prices, date, and currency from a single scan. For anyone who needs expense detail beyond just totals, this is a meaningful difference.

### Multi-currency handling

Currency detection is automatic — EasyReceipt reads the currency from the receipt rather than asking you to select it manually. Conversion uses real-time exchange rates, and both the original and converted amounts appear in your export. For travelers and international freelancers this works as advertised.

### Google Sheets export

This is an underrated feature. Most receipt apps export to CSV only — useful, but requires a manual import step. EasyReceipt pushes directly to Google Sheets, which means your expense data can live in a shared spreadsheet that your accountant or bookkeeper always has access to.

### Pricing honesty

The free tier is real: 10 AI-powered scans per month, 3-month receipt history. No watermarks. For casual users who want to try the app, this is a fair starting point. Export to CSV and Google Sheets is available on Pro and Power plans.

## What EasyReceipt Does Not Do

Being clear about limitations matters:

- **No offline scanning** — EasyReceipt requires an internet connection to process scans. In areas with poor connectivity, scans queue until you are back online.
- **No invoicing** — EasyReceipt is a receipt tracker, not a full accounting suite. It does not generate invoices, track payments, or reconcile bank accounts.
- **No team features** — there is no shared workspace, approval workflow, or multi-user account. Each account is individual.
- **10 scans/month on free** — active users will hit this limit quickly. The Pro plan at $5.99/month is the realistic entry point for regular use.

**How accurate is EasyReceipt's AI scanning?**
EasyReceipt reports 99%+ accuracy on standard printed receipts. Accuracy is lower on faded thermal paper, heavily crumpled receipts, or handwritten receipts. Scanning on the same day, while the receipt is flat and clean, produces the best results.

**Does EasyReceipt work internationally?**
Yes. Multi-currency support with real-time exchange rate conversion is available on all plans (limited on free, full on Pro and Power). The AI detects currency from the receipt automatically.
