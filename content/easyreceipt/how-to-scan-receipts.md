## 6 Tips for Accurate Receipt Scans

Getting 99%+ accuracy is easy if you follow these habits:

1. **Lay the receipt flat** — curved or crumpled receipts cause misreads
2. **Use good lighting** — avoid harsh shadows across the text
3. **Capture the full receipt in one frame** — do not cut off the top or bottom
4. **Shoot straight down** — hold the phone parallel to the receipt, not at an angle
5. **Clean your camera lens** — a smudged lens blurs fine print
6. **Smooth out thermal paper** — heat-printed receipts fade fast; scan them the same day
