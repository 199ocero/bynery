## Why Scan Receipts?

Gone are the days of stuffing paper receipts into a wallet. With modern AI, scanning a receipt takes seconds and gives you a permanent, searchable digital record.

## Getting Started

1. Open EasyReceipt on your phone
2. Tap the scan button
3. Point your camera at the receipt
4. The AI extracts merchant, date, items, and total automatically

## Tips for Best Results

- **Lay the receipt flat** on a contrasting surface
- **Ensure good lighting** — natural light works best
- **Capture the entire receipt** in one frame
- **Hold steady** — the AI needs a clear image

## What Gets Extracted?

EasyReceipt's AI recognizes:
- Merchant name and location
- Date and time of purchase
- Line items with quantities and prices
- Subtotals, tax, and final total
- Payment method

## Organizing Your Scans

After scanning, receipts are automatically:
- Categorized by merchant type
- Tagged with searchable keywords
- Synced to the cloud for backup
- Ready for export to CSV or PDF

Start scanning today and never lose a receipt again.
