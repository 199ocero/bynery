## The Psychology of Virtual Pets

Virtual pets have been motivating people for decades, from Tamagotchi to Nintendogs. Now, they're revolutionizing fitness.

## Why It Works

**Emotional Connection**
When your pet's happiness depends on your activity, walking becomes more than exercise—it becomes caretaking.

**Visible Progress**
Watching your pet evolve through life stages provides immediate, tangible feedback on your progress.

**Gentle Accountability**
Your pet doesn't judge, but their growth depends on your consistency. It's motivation without guilt.
