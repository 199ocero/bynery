## What Is Gamified Fitness?

Gamified fitness applies game design principles to exercise. Points, levels, achievements, and rewards turn workouts into engaging experiences.

## The Science Behind It

**Dopamine Release**
Achievements and progress triggers release dopamine, the brain's reward chemical. This creates positive associations with exercise.

**Clear Goals**
Games provide specific objectives and clear paths to achieve them. This clarity reduces decision fatigue.

**Instant Feedback**
Unlike traditional fitness where results take weeks, games provide immediate gratification.
