## Walking Reimagined

Walking is one of the best exercises for your health, but it can feel boring. Steppet changes that.

## The Magic Formula

**Choose Your Companion**
Start by selecting a virtual pet. Each pet has unique personality and evolution paths.

**Set Your Goal**
Customize your daily step target based on your fitness level and lifestyle.

**Watch Them Grow**
Every step counts toward your pet's evolution. Hit milestones to unlock new life stages.

## Features You'll Love

- **Real-time Tracking**: See your progress throughout the day
- **Streak Rewards**: Maintain consistency for special bonuses
- **Shareable Achievements**: Show off your progress with beautiful story posts

## Beyond Exercise

Steppet isn't just about fitness—it's about building a habit that lasts. Your pet depends on you, and that dependence creates powerful motivation.

## Get Started Today

Steppet is free on the App Store. Choose your pet and start your walking adventure.
